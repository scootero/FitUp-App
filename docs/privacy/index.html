FitUp Privacy Policy

This policy describes how FitUp collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

FitUp may collect the following types of information when you use the app:

  • Profile information — such as your display name, username, avatar, and other account details you provide.
  • Step and activity data — including daily steps and related fitness metrics used for challenges and stats.
  • Match and challenge data — including rivalries, match results, leaderboards, and participation in fitness challenges.
  • Device and app diagnostics — such as app version, device type, and crash or performance logs to help us improve reliability.
  • Feedback — including support messages, surveys, or other communications you send to us.

Health Data

With your permission, FitUp may access health and fitness data from Apple HealthKit (for example, step counts and related activity metrics).

HealthKit data is used only to power fitness challenges, stats, leaderboards, and other core app functionality. We do not use HealthKit data for advertising, marketing, or data brokerage.

FitUp does not sell HealthKit data. FitUp does not use HealthKit data for advertising.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and improve the FitUp app and its features.
  • Run fitness challenges, matches, and stats between you and your rivals.
  • Authenticate your account and keep the service secure.
  • Respond to support requests and feedback.
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve performance.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your personal information or HealthKit data.
  • We do not use HealthKit data for advertising or third-party marketing.
  • We do not share HealthKit data with data brokers.

Data Sharing

We may share limited information only as needed to run the service—for example, with cloud infrastructure providers that host our backend under contractual obligations to protect your data. Match and challenge information may be visible to other FitUp users as part of normal app features (such as leaderboards and rival stats).

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of FitUp and our users.

Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below.

You can find your FitUp display name and user ID inside the app under Profile → Account Deletion. Please include your FitUp display name or user ID with your request so we can locate your account.

We plan to add automated in-app account deletion in a future version of the app.

When your account is deleted, we will remove or anonymize your personal data in line with our retention practices, subject to any legal obligations to retain certain records.
